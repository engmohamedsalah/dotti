Three steps. Under five seconds.

Scan

Reads package.json, file tree, and existing configs. Detects your exact stack — frameworks, testing, database, styling, deployment.

Matches your stack against agent templates and rule sets. Each scored by relevance to YOUR code. Skip what you don't need.
